Professional Course Delivery Plan
Certificate III in Conservation and Ecosystem Management
Delivery Context: Classroom + Field | Duration: 36 Weeks (Not required weeks excluded)
________________________________________
1. Course Overview
This delivery plan translates the Training and Assessment Strategy (eTAS) into a structured, week by week teaching program. It ensures progressive skill development across workplace safety, plant and fauna identification, ecosystem management, seed collection and propagation, weed control, biosecurity, and environmental monitoring.
The plan supports:
•	Competency based assessment
•	Authentic field and industry aligned learning
•	Seasonal ecological relevance
•	Scaffolded theory to practice progression
________________________________________
2. Delivery Principles
Pedagogical approach
•	Applied, hands on learning
•	Field based contextual training
•	Progressive skill building
•	Emphasis on environmental stewardship and professional conduct
Assessment approach
•	Practical demonstrations
•	Logbooks and portfolios
•	Written reports and workplace style documentation
•	Knowledge checks and reflective activities
